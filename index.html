About Us

Marriage Hall Management System is a one stop application for the users which let the users to compare different halls as per their requirements by providing all the information related to that hall such as hall name, hall capacity, hall area, hall cost, providing services by the corresponding hall and their ratings too. Upon deciding about a particular hall, the user can simply book the hall by providing few details and then his/her booking is done.
